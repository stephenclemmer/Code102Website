What can you do with Pokemon?

Pokemon vary in their inherent strength. Once you catch a good one (or even a perfect one!) you can teach it new attacks and defenses so it will be able to win when battling other trainers. Trade your pokemon with friends to help one another build your menagerie. Use your Pokemon to clean up the mean streets around you by defeating the evil Team Go Rocket. Walk around the world with your favorite pokemon to unlock special challenges and abilities. This not only gets you fun prizes, but helps keep you fit.
